Steps you have to follow


1. Explore Our Trips

Take your time to navigate through our website and explore the wide variety of kayak tours we offer. Whether you’re looking for a peaceful paddle at sunset or an exciting wildlife tour, we have something for everyone. Each trip includes details, photos, and customer reviews to help you choose the perfect adventure.


2. Book Your Experience

Once you’ve found your ideal trip, head to the booking section. Fill out a simple reservation form with your personal information, desired date, and group size. If you have any questions or special requests, feel free to contact us—we're here to help!


3. Receive Confirmation and Details

After booking, you’ll receive a confirmation email with everything you need: your itinerary, meeting point, what to bring, and safety instructions. We'll make sure you’re fully prepared for your adventure. You can also check your reservation anytime by logging in to your account.


4. Arrive and Meet Your Guide

On the day of your trip, arrive at the designated location a few minutes early. You’ll be welcomed by our friendly and experienced guide, who will walk you through a short safety briefing and provide all the equipment you need, including life jackets and paddles.


5. Enjoy the Adventure!

Now the fun begins! Paddle through stunning natural landscapes, observe marine life, and experience the calm or excitement of the water—depending on your tour. Take pictures, laugh with your group, and enjoy every moment. At the end, you’ll return safely with unforgettable memories and probably a strong desire to come back soon!
